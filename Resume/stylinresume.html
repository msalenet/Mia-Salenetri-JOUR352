ABOUT ME

I am a storyteller, a researcher, an editor and a politics junkie. I am constantly learning, and I want to use my skills as a digital visual journalist to break down complex political issues using motion graphics and data visualization.
I grew up in a family that was as supportive of creativity as they were about knowledge.
During my time as a journalism student, I've noticed that context is essential to news. But more often than not, history, influences, implications and motivations are left to the footnotes, if not the wayside. I want to be able to provide that necessary background information.
